School Achievements

2020 California Distinguished School
We were one of the 323 schools in California to recieve the title of 2020 California Distinguished School.

This was a very big thing for our school. This award is given based on test scores, suspension rates, and conditions and climates. This title can be held for 2 years.

Prestigous National Blue Ribbon Award
Very important award won on March 20th, 2012.

On this day, students got to view the National blue ribbon flag and plaque. They also all heard their fellow peers sing the school song. After the assembly, one person from each flag was to put up a blue rubbon on a tree outside. Teachers got a free luncheon and cake while the students enjoyed Jamba Juice and extended recess. The award was given to Millikin based on academic results. The US's secratary of education came by to congratulate every school that won this award.
